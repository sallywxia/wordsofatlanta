About Words of Atlanta

"Words of Atlanta” is a data visualization on the Civil Rights Movement in Atlanta, specifically focusing on the year of 1960 and early 1961.

Mainly driven by students and led by Martin Luther King Jr., Atlanta’s Civil Rights Movement is fascinating period of time where people with different beliefs came together to avoid riots in Atlanta.

Realizing the complexity and depth of that specific period of history cannot be illustrated with a simple timeline, the main form of interaction is through quotes and words said and recorded by different groups of people to give a full context of the story as well as to draw light on the significance of the events.
